Please Don't Make a Wrong Guess at Pronouns

When someone is missgendered in an email or in verbal conversation, they can find this extremely upsetting.

Getting misgendered because somone guessed is painful because it reminds us that we don't nessesarily look like the gender we are.

So if you are not sure of which pronoun to use, then don't use them, or at least use gender neutral ones like they or them.

This goes for verbal communication as well.


Please Don't is a community project maintained at github.com/samathy/please-dont.
