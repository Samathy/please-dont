Please Don't Ask If I'm Having Surgery

My medical information is private.

Many people go under many different medical procedures for many different reasons.

Some trans people have Gender Confirmation Surgery1 but not all trans people do.

Some babies have circumcisions, but not all babies do.

When you go to the Doctors, you expect privacy, and if a stranger asks you what medical procedures you're having, you wouldn't want to answer

Trans people, whether they're having surgery or not, want the same privacy.

It's not appropriate to ask anyone, trans or cis, what genitals they have, so don't ask.

By asking whether we've had, or are going to have surgery, you are invalidating that privacy,

You are asking someone to disclose their private medical information to you,

That information is personal, and there should be no obligtion to share it, so; Please Don't Ask If I'm Having Surgery


1. Gender Confirmation Surgery is also known as Gender Recognition Surgery, Sex Reassignment Surgery, or Gender Reassignment Surgery. Please don't use the term "Sex Change".
