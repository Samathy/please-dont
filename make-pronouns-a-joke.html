Please Don't Make Pronouns a Joke


In English, we use the pronoun for a person that's correct for the self defined gender of the person (Remember, gender is not sex).

That might be He, She, They, Xe, Zie or many others. (See pronoun.is for a nice list of common ones.)

For cis people - their gender matches the gender assigned to them at birth (and probably matches their sex).

This makes it very easy for people to guess what pronouns to be using for a cis person since they can easily tell what your gender is by what you look like and sound like.

For a trans person, this probably isn't as easy for other people to tell. (This is a concept called 'Passing' )

Because its harder to guess our pronouns trans people often have to state what their pronouns are, many put them on their social media profiles.

So, why is it harmful when you make a joke out of stating our pronouns?

To many cis people, trans people are already a joke. Having to state your pronouns is already a joke.

By stating fake pronouns in your bio you're enforcing being trans as being a joke.

You're making it more okay to make fun of us and making trans people even more ostracised.
